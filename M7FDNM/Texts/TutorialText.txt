📜 TUTORIAL - Welcome to BitBlade!

You are a lone warrior in a cursed land overrun by monsters.  
To survive, you'll need strength, strategy, and a sharp blade.

🎮 Controls:
- Use the arrow keys to navigate the menu.  
- In each round, choose whether to attack or defend.

⚔️ Combat Basics:
- You and your enemy take turns.  
- Each attack has a chance to hit or miss.  
- Defend wisely — some monsters hit harder than others.

🧪 Tip:

🏆 Goal:
Defeat all the monsters without dying.

Good luck, warrior... your blade awaits!
